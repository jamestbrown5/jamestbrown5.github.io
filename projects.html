Spotted-Wing Drosophila x Microbe VOC

Other bacteria have been shown to produce attractive volatiles. These hidden players (yeast, fungi, bacteria) have also been implicated in affecting the interaction between insects and their hosts. These researchers sought to characterize the relative attractivenbess of SWD to multiple acetic acid bacteria using a two way olfactometer.
